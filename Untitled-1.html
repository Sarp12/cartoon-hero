About Sledge

Sledge is a character form the game Rainbow 6 Seige. He wears a gas mask that hides his identity and has a hammer to break almost any wall in the game

More about his hammer

It is a massive hammer that can destroy almost every wall(even rooftops!) in the game . This can be game changing when used correctly.You can also get really cool looking kills with it.

Some details about him

  1. He is voiced by Ryan Nicolls.
  2. His real name is Seamus Cowden.
  3. His position in the game is attacker.
  4. He is 6'3" (1.92m) tall.
